About Katie

A designer passionate about creating meaningful experiences that solve real problems

My Story

I'm a designer with over 8 years of experience creating digital experiences that matter. My journey started with a simple fascination with how things work and how they look. That curiosity evolved into a career dedicated to bridging the gap between beautiful design and intuitive functionality.

I believe that great design is problem-solving at its finest. It's not just about making things pretty—it's about understanding user needs, anticipating pain points, and crafting solutions that feel effortless. Every project I work on is an opportunity to create something that genuinely improves someone's day.

My background spans UI/UX design, branding, web design, and design strategy. I've had the privilege of working with startups, established corporations, and everything in between. This diverse experience has shaped my approach to design—flexible, collaborative, and always rooted in user research.
