Portfolio Projects

Mobile App Redesign

Role UI/UX Designer
Timeline 6 months
Tools Figma, Prototyping
Impact 45% increase in engagement
📱

Challenge

The existing financial app had complex navigation and an outdated interface that frustrated users. We needed to modernize the design while maintaining all functionality.

Solution

We conducted extensive user research, created new information architecture, and redesigned the interface with a focus on clarity and accessibility. The new design reduced task completion time by 30% and significantly improved user satisfaction scores.

Results

Post-launch, we saw a 45% increase in daily active users and a 28% improvement in app store ratings. User feedback was overwhelmingly positive regarding the new interface design.

Startup Brand Identity

Role Brand Designer
Timeline 3 months
Tools Adobe Creative Suite
Deliverables Brand Guide & Assets
💡

Challenge

A new tech startup needed a complete brand identity to stand out in a competitive market. They needed visual consistency across all touchpoints.

Solution

I developed a comprehensive brand strategy including logo design, color palette, typography guidelines, and a full design system that would scale across their growing product line.

Results

The brand identity has been successfully implemented across all marketing materials, web properties, and products. The startup has successfully raised Series A funding and attributes part of their success to the strong brand presence.
