About Me

Hello! I'm Katie

I'm a passionate designer with a keen eye for detail and a commitment to creating meaningful experiences. With expertise in UI/UX design, I combine aesthetics with functionality to solve real-world problems.

My design philosophy centers on understanding user needs, embracing simplicity, and creating solutions that are both beautiful and purposeful. I believe great design should be invisible—it should work so well that users don't think about it.
